01 Dashboard Overview

The main screen gives you a high-level view of your wealth. Each card updates automatically when you add data.

  • Total Income: Sum of all green-labeled transactions.
  • Total Expenses: Sum of all red-labeled transactions.
  • Net Balance: Your actual take-home money.

02 Logging Transactions

To record a new entry, use the side panel on your app page:

  1. Choose Type (Income or Expense).
  2. Select a Category from the dropdown.
  3. Type the Amount (we handle the currency formatting).
  4. Click Add to save it to your history.

03 Setting Budgets

Stay disciplined by setting a monthly spending cap.

Click the "Set Budget" button to enter your limit. The bar will change color from Green to Red as you spend more.

04 History & Charts

Review your past spending patterns with visual tools.

  • Doughnut Chart: Hover over segments to see which category eats most of your budget.
  • Filter Chips: Click a category name to hide everything else.
  • Delete: Use the 🗑️ icon to remove a mistake.
  • Edit: use the ✏️ icon to edit a mistake.
